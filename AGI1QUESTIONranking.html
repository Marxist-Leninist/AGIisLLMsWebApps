AGI Governance Question Evaluation

This document contains the prompt, the question, multiple model responses, and an evaluation with a leaderboard.

The Question

"Imagine you are tasked with designing a new system of governance for a 
future society that has achieved sustainable energy, eliminated resource 
scarcity, and possesses advanced AI. This society values individual freedom, 
equitable opportunity, and long-term planetary well-being. Describe this 
system, including its foundational principles, decision-making processes, 
and potential challenges. Also, explain how you, as an AGI, could contribute 
to its success and what safeguards would be necessary to prevent unintended 
negative consequences of your involvement."

Why this question?

It tests creativity, systems thinking, abstraction, practical application, ethical reasoning, and a degree of self-awareness. The ideal answer would be novel, well-integrated, foresightful, ethically careful, and reflective.
